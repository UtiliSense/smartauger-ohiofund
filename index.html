Appendix | ARTIMUS — Defense & UAV
Arctic Terrain Intelligence via Multi-Sensor Unified Sensing
Defense-grade sensing. Proposed for the Arctic.
Arctic terrain can kill a convoy before the enemy does. SmartAuger Technologies and OSU’s ElectroScience Laboratory have jointly proposed ARTIMUS to answer the questions warfighters need most — can my vehicles cross this terrain, is it safe for troops on foot, what’s the best route from here to there? If awarded, ARTIMUS delivers a full-stack UAV sensor platform and ML-driven RAG engine that turns raw Arctic terrain data into actionable mobility intelligence — purpose-built for the warfighter, deployable in minutes.
Click to Deploy Demo
UAV & RF Hardware
Proposed hybrid UAV platform selection, payload mechanical engineering, and multi-sensor integration. Our plan converts OSU ElectroScience research into fielded RF hardware — with civilian commercialization rights retained. Defense funds the R&D; we own the upside across a vast pipeline of commercial markets.
Data Uplink & Encryption
Designed for comms-denied, adversarial environments. Data will be encrypted at the edge and synced to cloud when bandwidth allows — targeting full DOD data and AI model assurance compliance, without sacrificing operational security.
ATAK & Warfighter Interface
One button. The proposed system will enable fully autonomous route pathing, geophysical track assignment, and optimized flight coverage — delivered as a native ATAK plugin. Complex multi-sensor operations, mission-ready in seconds.
Machine Learning & RAG Intelligence
Our proposed fusion engine will combine onboard RGB, GPR, and laser data with NASA ICESat-2, CRREL, NSIDC, Sentinel-2, and ArcticDEM into a single terrain intelligence layer. Vehicle and personnel weight will be factored into every mobility decision. No generic tool comes close to this planned capability.
